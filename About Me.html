Why Did I Start this Company?

My inspiration to start a business stemmed from a burning desire to work for myself, and a lifelong passion for the NBA and its players. I felt that genuine inspiration, principles, and empowerment were lacking in the NBA jersey industry. Thus, I created this website. My mission is to ignite inspiration in people, empowering them to push past limits and fight fearlessly.

What are the Goals for my Company?

With this company, I want to make a whole bunch of money. No joke. I like the NBA, and people will spend fortunes on jerseys. Easy life decision. Easy cash.

Why is this better than the offical NBA store?

Here, we have much better deals. Men's jerseys are only 50 bucks, children's are 30 bucks. Also, it's a solo owned company. Help a brother out.
